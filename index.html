Instructions

Welcome. It is your job to figure out the code word to escape. Try to escape in the fastest time.

  • You may use any methods at your disposal within the room. You're allowed to Google things if you think you need to, at your own discretion - but it probably won't help much.
  • You're allowed to write on all the puzzles.
  • Hints are available for each puzzle. Each puzzle is labelled on the back with which hint belongs to it. Each hint will add an extra minute to your time.
  • Any incorrect guesses will add an extra minute to your time.
  • The timer will begin as soon as you press START below.
